Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Peanut butter cookies are my all time favorite cookie. Maybe it is because of how simple they are to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter unsalted
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Combine flour and baking powder
  3. Mix butter and sugars
  4. Add peanut butter and mix
  5. Add the egg, vanilla, and flour and mix well
  6. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and place on a baking sheet
  7. Flatten the balls using a fork in a criss-cross pattern
  8. Bake for 12 minutes
  9. Allow cookies to cool
