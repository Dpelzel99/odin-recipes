Butter Chicken

Description

This recipe is one of my all time favorites. It is simple to make, especially when you have the simmer sauce in a jar... But one thing that makes this meal perfect is making sure you make the rice perfect as well. Some Naan bread and cilantro also add to the dish nicely.

Ingredients

For Chicken

  • 2 Lbs Chicken Thighs,skinless and skinless, cut into small chunks
  • 1 Tbsp oil

For Marinade

  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp cayenne
  • 1 tbsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 1/2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Sauce

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 2 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp cream

Instructions

  1. Start by mixing all the ingredients for marinade and add the chicken to it. Mix well, cover and set aside for 15-30 minutes while making the sauce.
  2. For the sauce, heat 1 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Add cinnamon, cloves, and garlic. Saute for a minute or until fragrant. Add onions and saute on high heat for 2-3 minutes till translucent. Add tomatoes. Mix once, and reduce the heat to a simmer and cover to cook for 10-15 minutes until tomatoes start breaking down.
  3. Turn off heat. Cool completely and blend mixture until smooth.
  4. In another pan, heat 1 tbsp of oil. Add chicken pieces and sear them on all sides for 2-3 minutes on high heat until there is a crust on them. Remove them and set aside.
  5. Transfer the puree back to the pan, add ketchup, salt, and 1/4 cup water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, and cover to cook for 15-20 minutes, the sauce should turn a deep orange.
  6. Add chicken and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in the cream.
